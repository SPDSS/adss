First level protections evaluation process

Notation:

  • metric(i, j) indicates the i-th metric for the j-th solution

Initialization phase:

  1. prune the protection instantiations (too much overhead, ...)
  2. prune the attack paths (too much difficult, ...)
  3. call the ACTC to extract the metrics for all the possible solutions containing only one PO
    • if we have n assets and m PIs, there are at most n * m solutions
    • example: n = 10 and m = 14 * 5 lead to 700 solutions -- if an ACTC compilation takes 1 minute, this lasts for 700 minutes = 11.7 hours
    • in a real scenario there are far less solutions because no protection can be applied everywhere
  4. generate some random, but valid, solutions (adequately spaced) and extract their metrics
  5. solve the linear approximation problem
